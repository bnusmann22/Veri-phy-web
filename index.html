The Problem

Merchants lose money
every single day.

Fraudsters have mastered fake payment alerts. One wrong release of goods can wipe out an entire day's profit.

Fake Payment Alerts
Fraudsters send fabricated SMS alerts that look exactly like real bank notifications — same format, same sender names, completely fake.
Most common fraud type
Edited Screenshots
WhatsApp and Instagram vendors receive manipulated payment screenshots from buyers. Amounts are changed after the fact with simple editing apps.
Rising in social commerce
Delayed Transfer Scams
A "reversal pending" trick — a real transfer is initiated but immediately reversed after goods are released. The merchant never gets paid.
POS agents most affected
Spoofed SMS Senders
Fraudsters spoof bank SMS sender IDs — your phone shows "GTBank" but the message came from a criminal. Indistinguishable to the naked eye.
Highly sophisticated attack
The Numbers Are Alarming

by a fake payment alert in the last 12 months.

Privacy & Security

Your privacy is
our foundation.

Veri-phy only reads payment alerts. Your personal messages, photos, and conversations are never accessed, stored, or shared.

Only Payment Alerts
Veri-phy uses intelligent pattern matching to identify only banking SMS alerts. Personal messages, WhatsApp, and email are completely untouched.
End-to-End Encrypted
All verification requests are encrypted using AES-256 before leaving your device. Even we cannot read the content of alerts you verify.
Realtime Verification
Verdicts arrive in under one second. Our infrastructure connects to bank APIs directly so you're never left waiting at the counter.
No Data Selling
Your transaction patterns and merchant activity are never sold to third parties. We are funded by merchants — not advertisers or data brokers.
NDPR Compliant
Fully compliant with Nigeria's Data Protection Regulation. Audited annually by independent security firms operating under CBN oversight.
Works Offline Too
Last known verdicts are cached locally. If you lose signal in the market, you still see the most recent verification status for each alert.
